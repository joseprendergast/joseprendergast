## Prototype tags

Repositories are tagged with topics so they can be filtered quickly:

- `important` - work I want people to notice first.
- `showcase` - public-facing projects that are presentable.
- `feature-project` - larger projects with more product intent.
- `prototype` - experiments, sketches, and one-shot builds.
- `one-shot` - quick builds or self-contained explorations.
- `ai`, `game`, `3d-game`, `poker`, `sabacc`, `web-app`, `chrome-extension`, `portfolio` - domain tags.

Some repositories are private while they are early, sensitive, or not ready to present publicly.
